Metadata for merging 400m grid units as of 2017.
'Seoul_2017_training. csv' is training data used for MTNN training.
'MTL_state_dict.pt' is a final model that saved through torch.jit.script()
Some GIS files that are difficult to convert to .csv have not been uploaded.
